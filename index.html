About Squashy Boy, who is he?

Squashy Boy is a Singaporean-based YouTuber/Content creator that used to makes videos related to osu!, but now makes videos about custom keyboards, building keyboards, reviewing them, or just any video related to keyboards.

Squashy Boy's Background and History

Squashy Boy used to make osu! videos from 2017 all the way to 2020. He started making his transition to keyboard content in 2019, with his first video about keyboards being Building my first Custom Keyboard. He addressed his hiatus on osu! videos in this video.

Squashy Boy's project keyboards:


The Gigguk Unit-02

The Gigguk Unit-02 was a keyboard built by Squashy Boy and was made for Gigguk, an anime YouTuber and costed $3000.

The keyboard parts are:

Case - Gigguk Unit-02 price is unknown, for it is commissioned

Switches - Holy Pandas $350

Stabilizers - Durock V2 Plate-mounted Stabilizers $21

Keycaps - GMK White on Black (WoB) $89.00


KBDFans MKII

The KBDFans MKII RGB PCwas Squashy Boy's dream keyboard at the time (Dec 24, 2020). This keyboard costed around SGD$1000, $701

The keyboard parts are:

Case - KBDFans MKII RGB PC $300

Switches - Holy Pandas $35 for 10 switches

Stabilizers - Durock V2 Plate-mounted Stabilizers $21

Keycaps - GMK Olivia $135


The Gentleman65

The Gentleman65 was Squashy Boy's new dream custom keyboard that costed around $600.

The keyboard parts are:

Case - Gentleman65 Suited Edition $265

Switches - Gateron Ink Blacks V2 $13 for 10 switches

Stabilizers - Durock V2 Plate-mounted Stabilizers $21

Keycaps - GMK Blue Samurai $159
